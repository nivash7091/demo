Jyoti HP Gas Agency

We are the authorised distributor of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited, serving the natives of Bihar state for more than a decade now. Established in the year 2003, we have come a long way , catering to all types of customers at grass root level. We are desirous of expanding and providing all solutions to your energy requirements and are committed to provide you clean and green energy, to work together towards a better future for our generations to come.

Jyoti HP is supplying 47.5 Kg LPG Cylinders to majority of the industrial areas in Bihar, to name a few Fatuah, Patna City, Bhabhua, Arrah.

Jyoti HP has pioneered the installation of the only 425 Kg LPG Cylinder Bank in Bihar, under the guidance of the great team of Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited.

We are also specialized in installation of Pipelines ( Cylinder Bank, Vaporizer , Gas Train) inclusive of the Civil construction.

Jyoti HP being one of the best Gas Agencies amongst its contemporaries, is committed to providing best in class customized services and after product sales, as per your business requirements.
